The Direct Connection

A staff position that makes intergenerational SSMUH developments physically inaccessible — by requiring elderly residents to climb 34 stairs from a lane to reach their home — is not a neutral enforcement of bylaw language. It is a design constraint that functionally excludes the population these units are intended to serve. If the outcome of "consistent" policy is housing that aging residents cannot navigate, the policy is producing the wrong result.
