Port Moody's Own Staff — On the Record, June 2024

Port Moody's planning staff described the SSMUH legislation to Council as a "broad-brush" approach that is "illogical and confusing." They are not wrong to note implementation challenges. But it is not coherent to criticize the Province for applying sweeping policy uniformly — and then apply your own blanket policy uniformly to individual applicants, in direct contradiction of the case-by-case flexibility your own guide promises.
