The Standard on Westmount Drive

Every home on Westmount Drive has access off of Westmount. Six have dual access — including one currently under construction. No homes on this street have only lane access. Yet staff insist that 1006 Westmount, at the highest point on the street with the steepest grade, must be the first.

A project off Mount Royal Drive was directed to lane-only access. The result: 11 vehicles between two homes, most parked on the street and in the neighbouring lane. It has created a blind corner and caused two accidents. It is the biggest complaint in the neighbourhood. This is what lane-only access produces — and staff want to make it the norm.
